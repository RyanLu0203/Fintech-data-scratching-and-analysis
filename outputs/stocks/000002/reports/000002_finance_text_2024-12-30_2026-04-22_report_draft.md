## Critical Reflection
The platform implements the required flow from raw market/news data to NLP sentiment, market money-flow pressure, RL trading decisions, and evaluation artifacts. FinBERT is attempted when the runtime can load the HuggingFace model; otherwise the pipeline logs a fallback and continues with lexicon/logistic signals so the end-to-end workflow remains runnable. If no reported net-flow column is available, daily net inflow/outflow is shown as an OHLCV-derived proxy rather than an exchange-reported capital-flow value.
